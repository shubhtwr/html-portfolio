3 Greatest Movies According to Shubham

My top 3 movies of all time

RRR

In the land of legends, where heroes stride,
"RRR" emerges, a cinematic pride.
With direction so masterful, it takes its flight,
Guided by a vision, a filmmaker's might.

BGM's symphony, a heartbeat's drum,
Weaves emotions deep, where battles are won.
Cinematography paints a vivid dream,
A visual feast, like a flowing stream.

In Rajamouli's world, a tale unfolds,
Of valor and friendship, as history holds.
"RRR" enchants with its every frame,
A cinematic gem, forever in fame.

Devdas

In the world of grandeur, where emotions collide,
"Devdas" weaves a saga, with love as its guide.
With set design so splendid, it's a sight to behold,
A world of opulence and tragedy untold.

A heart-touching story, like a melancholy song,
Of love unfulfilled, where hearts went wrong.
SRK, the star, in the lead role did shine,
With his passion and pain, in every single line.

From the grandeur of Paro's mansion so vast,
To the despair in Devdas' heart, unsurpassed.
A tale of love's trials, where destinies entwine,
"Devdas" enchants us with its timeless design.

Zero

In the galaxy of stars, a gem unseen,
"Zero" emerged, like a dream between.
Underrated, yet a tale so fantastic,
SRK's brilliance, in a role so elastic.

From the stature of a dwarf, he took his flight,
A character complex, with all his might.
A story of love, ambition, and chance,
"Zero" dared to dance its cosmic dance.

In the vastness of space, a unique quest,
A hero with heart, put to the test.
With Katrina's grace and Anushka's flair,
"Zero" crafted a world beyond compare.

Underrated, perhaps, but in hearts it found a place,
A cinematic marvel, a unique embrace.
For in its flaws, it still did shine bright,
"Zero" remains a star in the cinematic night.
